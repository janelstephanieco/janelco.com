Overview

Chanel's Library & Café is a cozy space inspired by slow mornings, warm drinks, and quiet moments spent with a good book.

Created as a place to pause from the noise of everyday life, Chanel's blends the comfort of a neighborhood café with the calm atmosphere of a library. From handcrafted coffee to small bites made for reading sessions and late night conversations, every detail is designed to feel soft, welcoming, and familiar. Inspired by the warmth and companionship of my dog named Chanel, the café invites guests to stay awhile, unwind, and make themselves at home.

The pomeranian mascot and supporting brand elements were hand drawn to bring a more personal and intimate feel to Chanel's Library & Café, inspired by my own dog, Chanel.
